Sphere vs Capsule detector

Constructor

new (swapped:Bool)

If swapped is true, the collision detector expects CapsuleShape and SphereShape for the first and second argument of SphereCapsuleDetector.detect. If swapped is false, the collision detector expects SphereShape and CapsuleShape instead.

Defined by Detector

detect (result:DetectorResult, shape1:Shape, shape2:Shape, transform1:Transform, transform2:Transform, cachedData:CachedDetectorData):Void

Computes the contact manifold of two shapes shape1 and shape2 with the transformations transform1 and transform2, and stores it to result. cachedData is used to improve performance of collision detection in some detectors.
